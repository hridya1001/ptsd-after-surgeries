Your Escalation Pathway — Symptom → Action → Contact
1
Something feels off
Mild concern or question
Pain slightly higher than expected, wound looks different, confused about medication timing, or simply anxious. Don't wait. Contact your named recovery nurse during working hours.
2
Escalate — call your GP
Worsening pain, fever, or swelling
Temperature above 38°C, wound becoming red or weeping, pain not controlled by prescribed medication, persistent nausea, or difficulty breathing on exertion. Call your GP today.
3
Emergency — act immediately
Chest pain, heavy bleeding, or collapse
Sudden severe pain, significant bleeding that won't stop, difficulty breathing at rest, confusion, or loss of consciousness. Call 999 or go to A&E immediately.
